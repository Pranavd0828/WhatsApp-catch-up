Core User Problem

Users receive long voice notes and miss important decisions, questions, and action items.

Product Sense Read

The feature wins if it makes long audio feel skimmable without breaking the user’s trust in WhatsApp’s private messaging model.

Why It Matters

  • Voice notes are convenient for senders but expensive for receivers.
  • Important asks are often embedded in casual speech.
  • The prototype turns audio into reviewable, editable, and actionable structure.
